Our Work

The secret of rug restoration is matching extremely closely how a carpet was woven originally.

Common Services:

  • Edge-binding along the sides of rugs
  • End-stopping on the end of rugs to halt unravelling
  • Reweaving holes caused by moths, pets, impact, furniture and age
  • Replacing bad old repairs
  • Re-piling (re-knotting) areas to recreate original design
  • Backing very worn carpets (conservation of antique pieces)
  • Stabilization of weak carpets (sewing up slits and brittle areas)
  • Reweaving kelims (flatwoven rugs)
  • Preparing rugs to be hung on walls
  • Custom-dying wool with natural dye materials

Additional services:

  • Making pillows of rugs that are damaged beyond repair
  • Embroidering design when reknotting is not an option(such as in extensive heavily worn areas)
  • Redying when repiling is not an option
  • Dying old repairs to match the rest of the rug
  • Cutting and rejoining rugs to fit a particular space or to get rid of irrepairable damage
  • Cutting holes for electrical cords
  • Some stain removal
  • Moth-proofing consultations
